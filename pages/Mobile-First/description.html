Back in 2011, Hayden got handed down an old camera from his Uncle Ricky. We took that camera and went straight out into the nearby forest and started experimenting with it. We soon learned how to capture the natural beauty of earth and portray it the way it's supposed to be - we never looked back. In 2017, we finally decided it was time to take our passion for photography and videography, love for the outdoors and our addiction to adventure, and start this business. Our mantra is simple, You like the road less traveled, and so do we. We want to capture your adventures and provide you a magnanimous portrayal of your memories. -Griffin C. Stiens (CEO)
